Using the LJ Cut tag

LiveJournal features a special tag known as the LJ Cut tag. This tag allows you to "snip" portions of your journal entries to keep them from taking up too much space on your journal page (or other users' friends pages). Xjournal features support for this tag as well.

There are two ways to use the LJ Cut tag:

  • The first is to select the text you want to hide and then select the LJ Cut option from the HTML menu. Enter your desired text in the Cut Label field and click the OK button.

  • The second method is to place your cursor where you would like the cut text to appear. Next, select the LJ Cut option from the HTML menu. Enter your desired text in the Cut Label field and then enter your desired text in the Hidden Text field. Finally, click the OK button.
