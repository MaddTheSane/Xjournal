Viewing, editing, and deleting past entries

Xjournal's History browser allows you to easily view and modify your journal entries. To open the browser, select the History option from the Window menu. It is recommended that you use the Refresh button to refresh your History view. By using the Download button, Xjournal will store your entries locally for faster use.

To work with a past entry, select it using the year, month, day, and subject selectors. You can also search for a previous entry using the Search field. Once selected, simply click the Edit or Delete buttons as appropriate. You can also view the entry live by clicking the Open in Browser button.
