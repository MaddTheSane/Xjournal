Adding and Removing Friends and Groups

To work with your friends and communities, open the Friends window by selecting Friends from the Window menu. Along the left-hand side you will see the group pane, which is similar to iTunes' Library. On the right, upper side, you will find the member pane, which lists users and communities who are members of the container selected in the group pane. Finally, beneath the member pane you will find several panes for viewing a user's settings, recent entries, and LiveJournal user information.

Adding a new user is as easy as selecting Add Friend from the Friends menu. Enter the friend's LiveJournal username and then click OK. To delete a user from your friends list, select All Friends in the group pane. Next, select the desired user in the member pane. Once you've selected Delete Friend from the Friends menu, the user will be removed from your LiveJournal friends list and all groups.

To add a new group, select Add Group from the Friends menu. Adding one of your friends to your new group is as simple as dragging him from the members list and dropping him in the desired group. Removing a friend from a group is much the same. Select the group from the group pane and the friend in the member pane. Then, select Remove Friend from Group from the Friends menu. To delete a group, select it in the group pane and then select the Delete Group from the Friends menu.

Once you are done making changes, be sure to click the Save button to save your changes and upload them to the LiveJournal servers.
