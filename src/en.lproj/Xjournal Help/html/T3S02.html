Formatting music entries

If you would like to format the Music field, you can configure Xjournal's preferences to do it automatically. First, select the Preferences option from the Xjournal application menu. Click the Music toolbar icon to view Xjournal's music preferences.

The music preferences are divided into three main sections. The first section allows you to specify Prefixes and Suffixes for the data retrieved from iTunes. The second section allows you to specify what fields are displayed, what order they are displayed, and what is used to separate them. Finally, the third section shows an example of what your settings will produce.

TIP: You can use HTML in these fields to create more customised music listings.
