Xjournal Help


Overview

Xjournal is a Mac OS X client for the LiveJournal.com weblog service. Xjournal has many features, including:

  • With offline mode, you can create multiple journal entries without an Internet connection. Save them to disk to work on later and then post them to LiveJournal when you get back on the Internet!

  • Xjournal reads your bookmarks from Safari and Address Book and puts them on a palette for easy insertion into your journal entries.

  • Using the History Browser, you can browse through all of your LiveJournal entries. Display them right in Xjournal or use the quick-link buttons to open them in your web browser for viewing or editing.

  • The Friends tools allow you to easily edit your friends and friend groups right in Xjournal. Use the notification preferences to keep you updated when your friends post, with a simple Dock indicator, a dialog box, or even one of your system sounds.

  • Xjournal's Glossary palette keeps snippets of text for quick drag and drop into your journal entries.

  • Using the Music preferences, you can have Xjournal auto-detect your currently playing song in iTunes and format it for posting with your journal entries.
