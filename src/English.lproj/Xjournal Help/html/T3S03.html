Linking to the iTunes Music Store

Xjournal allows you to have your detected music listed at the bottom of your entries. The listing will have each section of the song's information as a clickable link to that particular item in the iTunes Music Store. To enable this feature, select the Preferences option from the Xjournal application menu. Click the Music toolbar icon to view the Xjournal music preferences.

Under the Itunes Music Store heading, select the item specifying Links to iTune Music Store in body of post option. Optionally, you can specify HTML to preface the link text (e.g. Music:).
