Using the Bookmarks panel

The Xjournal Bookmarks panel retrieves your bookmarks from Apple's Safari web browser as well as the Mac OS X Address Book. This panel allows you to easily drag and drop URLs into your journal entries. To do so, first open the Bookmarks panel by selecting the Bookmarks option from the Window menu. Then, drag the desired bookmark from the panel and drop it in your entry at the desired location for the URL.

TIP: You can also drag an item from the Bookmarks panel into the HREF field of the Insert Link sheet.
